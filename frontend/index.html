Model Architecture

Supervised learning (Xception / ViT) is the primary classifier. An autoencoder provides a second signal for novel deepfakes. Their outputs are fused by a weighted ensemble. Click any node for details.

Xception

Industry-standard CNN benchmarked on FaceForensics++. Excels at detecting spatial GAN artifacts and blending boundaries in manipulated faces.

EfficientNet-B4

ViT / LSTM (3D CNN)

Unsupervised · Support

Autoencoder

Trained only on real faces — learns the manifold of authentic appearance. High reconstruction error at inference signals a likely deepfake, even one never seen before.

Zero-shot generalisation Novel fakes
Unsupervised · Support

GAN Discriminator

Adversarially trained to distinguish real from generated. Provides an independent realness score that complements the supervised classifier output in the ensemble.

Adversarial training Realness score
Output · Fusion

Weighted Ensemble

Combines outputs of all models using learned weights. Supervised score drives the primary signal; autoencoder / GAN boost confidence on unseen manipulation techniques.
